During Week 3, the focus was on understanding the structure and implementation of recursive algorithms. You explored the fundamental concept of algorithmic recursion, which involves a function calling itself to solve smaller instances of a problem. By the end of the week, you should be able to trace and write recursive algorithms that enables you to understand and debug recursive processes effectively. You also practiced converting iterative algorithms into their recursive counterparts using pseudocode, reinforcing their understanding of both approaches. Additionally, you calculated the time complexity of recursive algorithms, gaining insight into their efficiency and performance compared to iterative solutions. This combination of theoretical and practical skills provided a comprehensive understanding of recursion in algorithm design.

The recursive definition of the factorial function is provided:

* The factorial of n is equal to n times the factorial of (n-1)
* The factorial of (n-1) is equal to (n-1) times the factorial of (n-2)
* The factorial of 0 is 1

To analyze the running time of the recursive algorithm, the following recurrence equation is defined:

t(n) = c4 + t(n-1)

where c4 is a constant.

The recurrence equation describes the running time of the problem of size n in terms of the running time of a smaller input (n-1).

This type of equation appears every time we need to calculate the running time of a recursive algorithm, and it's essential to learn how to solve them.

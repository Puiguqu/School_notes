The video covers several key concepts, including:

* **Statefulness**: An object's state refers to its internal data and how it changes over time.
* **Static functions**: Static functions belong to a class or module and do not have access to an object's state. They are essentially self-contained blocks of code that perform specific tasks.
* **Non-stateful things**: Non-stateful things, on the other hand, do not maintain internal data and exist solely for their intended purpose.

Some screen readers may require using CTRL in conjunction with the alt key Welcome to week 16. This week's topic is accessibility, which is a very important aspect of software design. Let us have a look at the learning objectives here to see what we're going to cover. Firstly, we're going to learn how we can differentiate between accessibility and usability, which are both related, but I think you can differentiate based on the target audience. Secondly, we are going to be able to use accessibility evaluation tools to identify problems with systems that we've built and that other people have built, especially websites in this instance. Finally, we're going to have a little look at some of the legal aspects of accessibility in the U.K. especially when we're creating public facing systems.

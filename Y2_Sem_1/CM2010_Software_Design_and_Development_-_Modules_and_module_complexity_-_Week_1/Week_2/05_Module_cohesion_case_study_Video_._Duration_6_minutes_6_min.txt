Here is a summary of the module cohesion case study:

**Objective:** To analyze the cohesion within a specific module and identify different types of cohesion present.

**Module:** The module analyzed was a part of a program related to a paint application, specifically handling the 'Toolbar'.

**Types of Cohesion:**

1. **Class Cohesion:** The functions inside the module were related to the dataset of tools in the 'Toolbar', indicating strong class cohesion.
2. **Logical Cohesion:** Although not explicitly stated as an issue, it was mentioned that some functions could be considered logically similar due to their functionality.
3. **Consequential Cohesion:** There was a clear relationship between the functions, with each function building upon the previous one (e.g., 'addTool' calling 'addToolIcon').

**Reasoning:**

* The module was well-structured and focused on 'Toolbar' related functionality.
* All functions within the module were related to the dataset of tools in the 'Toolbar', indicating strong cohesion.
* There was a clear logical flow between the functions, with each function building upon the previous one.

**Conclusion:**

The module exhibited good cohesion due to its well-defined purpose and consistent structure. The functions within the module worked together logically and sequentially, resulting in a cohesive class. This example demonstrates how to analyze module cohesion and identify different types of cohesion present in a specific module.

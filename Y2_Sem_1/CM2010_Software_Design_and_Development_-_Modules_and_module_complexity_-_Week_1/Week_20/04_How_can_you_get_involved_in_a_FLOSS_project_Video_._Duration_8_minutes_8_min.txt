**Introduction to Free Libre Open Source Software (FLOSS)**

The speaker introduces FLOSS and explains that it means the source code is open, available for people to work on, and develop. They discuss different approaches to FLOSS and licenses.

**Getting Involved in FLOSS**

The speaker talks about why you should get involved in FLOSS projects and provides some practical tips on how to do so:

1. Look at the contribute URL to see what the project maintainers are asking people to contribute.
2. Research typical contributions made by other contributors and read the commentary around those contributions.

**Conclusion**

The speaker concludes that getting involved in FLOSS projects can be a great way to contribute and learn new skills.

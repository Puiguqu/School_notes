When you have completed this topic, if you would like to read in more depth about any of the content covered, we recommend the following readings. They will deepen your understanding in some areas but are not required in order to pass the module. Ceri, S. et al, Chapter 1: Section 1.2 HTTP and HTML: the foundation of web technology , which underpin web applications. This section then discusses client-side scripting and cascading style sheets, which are the key technologies of our presentation tier. Section 1.4 SQL: the Structured Query Language for relational databases , which gives a high-level explanation of relational databases that we will look at in more as the key technology of our data tier. Section 1.5.4 Server-side scripting , which is an approach to creating dynamic page content. This will be an important part of our application tier.

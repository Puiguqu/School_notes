You learn how to show your design in an entity relationship diagram and how you can make relations in a relational database by using a foreign key. You will also learn about SQL JOIN statements. SQL JOIN merges information from multiple related tables and makes advanced querying possible. You will have the chance to practice SQL JOIN in MySQL shell to define new databases with tables including foreign keys. Then you will be introduced to the different possible types of associations or relations between tables in a relational database. You will learn about the problem of many-to-many associations and how junction tables or bridge tables can be added to an entity relationship diagram to solve the problems of many-to-many associations.

The University of London is piloting an online service called Studiosity to support you with your academic writing. The service can also be used for additional support on topics you might need a refresher on during the course of your study (for example, basic maths questions). Through Studiosity, you will be able to upload drafts of essays, reports, and other written assessment types. Experts in academic writing and detailed feedback will respond to you, explaining how your work can be improved....

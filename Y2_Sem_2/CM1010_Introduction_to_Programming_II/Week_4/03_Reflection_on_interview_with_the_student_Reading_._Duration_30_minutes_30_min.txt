1. After reviewing the interview with Evan, students must take time to deeply reflect on their current progress with the drawing application project.
2. This reflection should guide the student in integrating insights from Evan's work into their own implementation.
3. Students are encouraged to compare Evan's built features, such as the color picker and line tool, to their own design.
4. A key consideration is whether existing or new features from Evan's application inspire enhancements to the core functionality.
5. Evan demonstrated both simple and advanced tools, suggesting students should aim for similar levels of complexity in their own design.
6. Analyzing how implementing sophisticated tools can benefit the project planning process is crucial for students.
7. The technical scope must also consider the requirements for implementing more complex p5.js features, such as three-dimensional graphics (3D).
8. To maximize the benefit of the midterm assignment, students must detail a comprehensive implementation plan.
9. Consideration should be given to utilizing various planning techniques to support the successful implementation of advanced features.
10. Effective planning requires leveraging brainstorming sessions and systematically iterating through different design concepts.
11. This iterative process is expected to lead to a significantly improved and optimized final project outcome.
12. Students must establish clear metrics for assessing their current project progress and ensuring they remain on track.
13. Defining how they will maintain the required standard and complete the project on time is essential for accountability.
14. The evaluation process, mirrored in Evan's video, requires students to reflect on elements they would change in their final submission.
15. It is therefore advisable to begin tracking one's thinking and project progress immediately to aid future self-assessment.
16. Specific items to document include all initial brainstorming designs and preliminary sketches.
17. Students must also keep records of their sources of inspiration and any technical help received during the process.
18. Furthermore, a continuous, regular logging of progress is mandatory documentation required for grading.
19. These planning and documentation requirements must be addressed at both the midterm and end-of-term assignment submissions.
20. The overall process requires students to maintain a clear link between design goals and technical feasibility within p5.js.
21. The core technical details—including color pickers and line tools—must be critically examined for possible redesigns.
22. Addressing the challenge of advanced features, such as 3D, requires detailed investigation of p5.js limitations and capabilities.
23. Maximizing the midterm plan's benefit means showing the journey of planning, not just the final scope.
24. Continuous evaluation preparation involves logging not only achievements but also perceived difficulties or mistakes.
25. By meticulously keeping track of these records, students prepare thoroughly for the final reflection required upon submission.

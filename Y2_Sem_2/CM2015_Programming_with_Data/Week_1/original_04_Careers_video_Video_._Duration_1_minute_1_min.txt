To support the global nature of our students we have an event series where we focus on regional information. In them we dedicate time to illuminate what current employment and sector trends in specific parts of the world are and each session focuses on one region and looks to provide a general understanding of what that location is really prioritising. We focus a lot of our efforts to provide this bespoke information including a large variety of international vacancies as hosted on our Careers Connect system. By attending these sessions we're sure our students will be able to apply to their desired roles even more successfully and if our students are unable to attend our webinars we have an extensive library of recordings within our virtual learning environment where they can watch or listen back at their convenience. By doing this students will gain a deeper understanding of those areas and will be in a better position than they were before attending to take the next step in their career development.

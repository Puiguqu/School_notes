In this video, we looked at two techniques to filter data, value-based and time-based filtering techniques. Value-based filtering techniques make use of comparison operators such as equal to greater than or less than to filter and retrieve specific values. Time-based filtering involves changing date from a string format to a date-time format and setting it as an index of the data frame.

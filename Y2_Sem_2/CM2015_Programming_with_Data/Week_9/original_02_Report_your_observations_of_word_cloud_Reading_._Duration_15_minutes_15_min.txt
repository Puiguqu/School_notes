Reflect on the word cloud created from the programming activity. Here are some reflective prompts: Themes: What are the largest, and second largest words in the word cloud? From these, what can you infer as the theme of the data? Sentiment and tone: Based on the prominent words in the word cloud, what overall tone or sentiment does the data convey? Data quality: Are there important terms that appear less frequently than expected? Are there any common words or irrelevant terms (like 'and' 'also') that distract from key insights?

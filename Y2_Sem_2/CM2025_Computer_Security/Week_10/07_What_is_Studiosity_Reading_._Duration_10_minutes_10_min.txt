# What is Studiosity?

. Duration: 10 minutes
10 min - Summary

URL: https://www.coursera.org/learn/uol-cm2025-computer-security/supplement/NbYMj/what-is-studiosity

The University of London is piloting a specialized online service called Studiosity. This platform is designed specifically to support students with their academic writing needs. Studiosity provides assistance far beyond just writing support. The service can also be utilized for refresher support on various academic topics. For example, students can request help with basic mathematical questions. Through the use of Studiosity, users are able to upload various written assessment types. These types include essays, comprehensive reports, and other required written drafts. Upon uploading material, expert resources in academic writing will provide detailed feedback. These experts explain exactly how the student's submitted work can be substantially improved. Students should read on to access Studiosity and learn more about the service's features. One primary way to use the tool is by uploading an assignment draft. This upload function allows for fast feedback, helping students check if they are on the correct trajectory. Students can also choose to chat live for instant feedback. The live chat function supports writing questions, science queries, and maths problems. Students can also ask general study questions that need attention. Specific student questions illustrate the platform’s utility. One potential query is: "This assignment needs to be structured as a report, can you see if this reads ok?" Another concern is determining the quality of the conclusion, asking: "Does my conclusion make any sense?" A complex issue addressed is handling differing viewpoints, like: "I'm making two very different arguments here, can you give any feedback?" Furthermore, academic troubleshooting is possible, such as when a student writes: "I keep getting the wrong answer for x, but my working looks ok - can we chat through it?" These multiple examples demonstrate the wide range of assistance offered. The Studiosity advisors are prepared to help with all these types of detailed academic inquiries.

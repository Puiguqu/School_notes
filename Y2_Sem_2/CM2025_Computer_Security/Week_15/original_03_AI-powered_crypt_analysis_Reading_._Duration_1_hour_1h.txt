The purpose of this reading is to explore how machine learning can automate the task of identifying encryption algorithms from ciphertext. Read: Radhika, K., S. Verma, R. Sathya, T. Kathirvel, K. H. Vishnu and N. M. Rajan ‘AI powered crypt-analysis for identification of encryption algorithm’ in 2024 4th International Conference on Sustainable Expert Systems (ICSES) (Washington, DC: IEE, 2024) pp.1880–1884.

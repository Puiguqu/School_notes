# Reviewing different types of lightweight cryptography
Reading•
. Duration: 1 hour
1h

URL: https://www.coursera.org/learn/uol-cm2025-computer-security/supplement/nWK9b/reviewing-different-types-of-lightweight-cryptography

In the IoT Security video, we saw how standard cryptography like AES can overwhelm resource-constrained devices. This creates demand for lightweight cryptography that balances security with efficiency. This reading reviews the 10 finalist algorithms from NIST’s standardisation process, comparing their energy use, performance and suitability for IoT and embedded systems. Read: Buchanan, W.J. and L. Maglaras ‘Review of the NIST light-weight cryptography finalists’ in 2023 19th International Conference on Distributed Computing in Smart Systems and the Internet of Things (DCOSS-IoT). (Pafos: Cyprus, 2023) pp.469–474. for the article in the Online Library . As you read, focus on: how the algorithms address constraints such as power, speed and memory what trade-offs emerge in performance across different hardware platforms.

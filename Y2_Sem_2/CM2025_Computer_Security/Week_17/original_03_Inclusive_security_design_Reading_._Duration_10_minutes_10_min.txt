# Inclusive security design
Reading•
. Duration: 10 minutes
10 min

URL: https://www.coursera.org/learn/uol-cm2025-computer-security/supplement/8slK6/inclusive-security-design

Inclusive security design means building systems that serve everyone regardless of ability. When systems overlook accessibility, they can exclude users, encourage risky workarounds or even introduce new vulnerabilities. The following NCSC article emphasises that accessibility isn’t just ethical – it’s a cybersecurity imperative.

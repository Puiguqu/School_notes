# Activity – EDI failures in cybersecurity and technology systems
Reading•
. Duration: 2 hours
2h

URL: https://www.coursera.org/learn/uol-cm2025-computer-security/supplement/q6uZi/activity-edi-failures-in-cybersecurity-and-technology-systems

In this activity, you will investigate and reflect on real-world failures in EDI within cybersecurity and technology systems. Record your findings in your study journal. 1. Research: facial recognition systems misidentifying minorities discriminatory hiring algorithms under-representation in cybersecurity leadership roles. 2. For each area, write an analysis that covers: a summary of the case/example the causes of the EDI failure (e.g. biased data, lack of oversight, cultural/systematic issues) the impact on individuals, organisations and society the lessons that can be learned. 3. Conclude your analysis with a reflection that covers: why addressing EDI matters in cybersecurity and technology the practical steps that could be taken to avoid such failures in the future.

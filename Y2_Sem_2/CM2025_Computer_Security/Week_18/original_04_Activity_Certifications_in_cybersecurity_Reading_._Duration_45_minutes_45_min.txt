# Activity – Certifications in cybersecurity
Reading•
. Duration: 45 minutes
45 min

URL: https://www.coursera.org/learn/uol-cm2025-computer-security/supplement/E9v1U/activity-certifications-in-cybersecurity

Certifications can play a key role in the cybersecurity industry. While degrees and hands-on skills are essentials, employers often look for certifications as proof that candidates meet recognised industry standards as outlined in the following article. Read: Cyber Zone Global ‘ The Importance of Cybersecurity Certifications in Digital World ’ (2024). If this link is broken, please let us know via the Student Portal . Now do some research into which cybersecurity certifications your region or country values the most. job boards (e.g. LinkedIn, Indeed or local sites) for cybersecurity roles and note which certifications appear most often in job requirements or preferences. Check local professional bodies or training providers to see what they recommend. Summarise your findings in your study journal (150–200 words), answering the following questions. Which certifications are most in demand locally? What kinds of roles or career levels are they linked to? Do they focus on general knowledge or specialist skills ?

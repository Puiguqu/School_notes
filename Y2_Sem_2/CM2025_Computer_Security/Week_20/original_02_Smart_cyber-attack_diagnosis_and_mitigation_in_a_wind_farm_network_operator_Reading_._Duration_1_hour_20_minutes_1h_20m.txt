# Smart cyber-attack diagnosis and mitigation in a wind farm network operator

This reading shifts our focus from software supply chains to critical infrastructure cybersecurity. In particular, it examines how a wind farm operator can use smart diagnosis and mitigation frameworks to detect and respond to cyber attacks on control networks. Read: Badihi, H., S. Jadidi, Z. Yu, Y. Zhang and N. Lu ‘Smart cyber-attack diagnosis and mitigation in a wind farm network operator,' IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics 19(9) pp.9468–9478. for the article in the Online Library . As you read, consider: how cyber attacks on industrial control systems (ICS) differ from IT system breaches like SolarWinds or Uber what role smart diagnosis and automated response can play in ensuring a safe, continuous operation of critical energy infrastructure.

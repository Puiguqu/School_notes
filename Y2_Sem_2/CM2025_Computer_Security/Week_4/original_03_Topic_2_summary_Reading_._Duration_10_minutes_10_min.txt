This topic has focused on how organisations build strong cybersecurity foundations through structured governance, risk management and compliance practices. So far, you have explored: cybersecurity frameworks and standards. You have studied the major frameworks (e.g. NIST, ISO/IEC 27001, COBIT, PCI DSS) and how internal policies like access control or incident response policies put these into practice legal, ethical and professional responsibilities. You have considered the role of legal compliance (e.g. GDPR), professional conduct and ethical reasoning in guiding cybersecurity decisions frameworks and ethical reasoning. Through case studies and scenarios (e.g. Equifax, SolarWinds), you have practised classifying risks, applying standards and evaluating the outcomes of different responses. By completing interactive elements such as matching exercises, breach analysis activities and scenario-based discussions, you are understanding how abstract concepts apply in the real-world and about the balance between regulations, risk and business agility.

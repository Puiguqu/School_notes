# Delving deeper into understanding IDS
Reading•
. Duration: 45 minutes
45 min

URL: https://www.coursera.org/learn/uol-cm2025-computer-security/supplement/6Vqxv/delving-deeper-into-understanding-ids

This article takes the IDS idea further. Instead of one system doing the work, it looks at how detection can happen across a whole network, with systems sharing information and making joint decisions. Think teamwork but for cybersecurity. Read: Kemmerer, R.A. and G. Vigna ‘Intrusion detection: A brief history and overview’, Computer 35(4) 2002, suppl.27–30. for the article in the Online Library . While reading, consider: what could go wrong when multiple systems try to work together how trust and accuracy affect this kind of collaboration would this help speed up responses to threats, or just add complexity?

# Authentication and authorisation
Reading•
. Duration: 50 minutes
50 min

URL: https://www.coursera.org/learn/uol-cm2025-computer-security/supplement/OzeYz/authentication-and-authorisation

Building on your understanding of access control models and the role of intrusion detection and prevention systems (IDS/IPS), this paper explores multi-factor authentication (MFA) as a complementary mechanism for strengthening system security. While access control defines who can enter and IDS/IPS monitor for suspicious activity once systems are in use, MFA reinforces the initial gateway by demanding more than just a password for login. Read: ALSaleem, B.O. and A.I. Alshoshan ‘ Multi-Factor Authentication to Systems Login’ , in 2021 National Computing Colleges Conference (NCCC). (New York: IEEE, 2021) pp. 1-4. for the article in the Online Library . As you read, consider the following questions. How does MFA strengthen system login security compared to traditional password-only access control? What challenges might organisations face when implementing MFA, and how do these relate to usability and system integration?

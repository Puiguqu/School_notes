# Summarise programme regulations
Reading•
. Duration: 30 minutes
30 min - Summary

URL: https://www.coursera.org/learn/uol-cm2045-professional-practice-for-computer-scientists/supplement/O2Yoq/summarise-programme-regulations

This activity requires summarizing the BSc Computer Science programme regulations into a concise guide for new students. These regulations establish the essential academic framework, outlining necessary 'dos and don'ts' for the entire journey. The purpose is to elucidate rules governing course requirements, assessment criteria, and general academic policies. Crucially, this exercise aims for the student to develop skills in extracting critical information from complex technical documentation. This skill set is noted as being highly valuable in both professional and academic contexts. Students must locate the specific programme regulations on the University of London’s Academic regulations page. This process involves scrolling down to the current year and then selecting 'Undergraduate' to access Computer Science regulations. The summarized guide must be highly accessible, functioning as a quick reference for new students. It must remain short, ideally fitting within two pages. To ensure clarity, the summary must utilize specific structural elements, including headings and subheadings. Suggested sections include Modules and credits, Assessment and grading, and Special circumstances. The presentation must emphasize conciseness, utilizing bullet points or numbered lists where possible. Consideration should also be given to adopting a table format for maximum clarity and readability. The guide should allow a new student to grasp the key rules in mere minutes, thereby building confidence. The technical focus of the summary must be on identifying crucial information within the source document. Key areas of focus include detailed assessment guidelines and core course requirements. Furthermore, the summary must clearly address principles of academic integrity. A sophisticated balance between program flexibility and structural constraints must also be presented. The ultimate goal is to successfully break down a complex, technical document into an easily digestible format. The final summary must accurately reflect all fundamental policies contained within the regulations. This structured approach ensures all procedural steps and academic requirements are covered. The resultant guide serves as a foundational resource for understanding academic expectations. It acts as a preliminary orientation tool, clarifying academic expectations immediately. The emphasis remains on pedagogical clarity and functional usability for the target audience.

# Activity – Analysing a security breach in Mr. Robot
Reading•
. Duration: 1 hour
1h - Summary

URL: https://www.coursera.org/learn/uol-cm2045-professional-practice-for-computer-scientists/supplement/o1NwU/activity-analysing-a-security-breach-in-mr-robot

*Mr. Robot* is an American TV show centered on a cybersecurity engineer and hacker recruited by a hacktivist group called 'fsociety.' This group aims to destabilize corrupt corporations through sophisticated cyber attacks. The core activity requires participants to analyze a security breach depicted in the show. The primary objectives are educational: applying cybersecurity principles, analyzing breach causes, and developing critical thinking skills. Participants must identify systemic failures and propose comprehensive mitigation strategies. Students are given recommendations, such as Season 1, Episode 5 (fsociety hacks Steel Mountain) or Season 1, Episode 1 (Elliot hacks Evil Corp). If these clips are inaccessible, the assignment permits using any fictional scenario depicting a cyber breach from media. Careful analysis requires paying attention to three key areas of failure. First, technical vulnerabilities include weaknesses like weak passwords, unpatched software, or insecure network configurations. Second, human factors address the role of error or social engineering, such as phishing or insider threats. Third, process failures examine shortcomings in security policies, procedures, or incident response plans. The subsequent report must summarize the breach and identify these systemic technical, human, and process failures. Specific examples from the selected clip must support this analysis. Next, participants must propose realistic mitigation strategies for the identified failures. These strategies must cover both technical solutions, like stronger authentication or IDS, and non-technical measures, such as employee training or awareness campaigns. Finally, the report requires a reflective component addressing real-world relevance. Students must consider how *Mr. Robot*'s breaches relate to current cybersecurity threats. Reflection must also tackle the human element, recognizing that fatigue or emotional states compromise security. Furthermore, the analysis must address ethical dilemmas raised by hacking, privacy, and security in the show. This structured activity bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. By analyzing a fictional breach, one gains a nuanced understanding of cybersecurity challenges and solutions.

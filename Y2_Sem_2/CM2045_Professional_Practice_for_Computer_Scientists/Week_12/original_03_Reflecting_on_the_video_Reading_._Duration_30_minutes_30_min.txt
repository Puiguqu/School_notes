After you’ve watched the video with Carols Russell at Ternium, reflect on the following questions in your study journal. How can individuals and organisations better protect themselves against evolving cybersecurity threats, such as AI-powered attacks and social engineering? Why is compliance and ethical decision-making critical in cybersecurity, and how can transparency improve trust and security? How do emerging technologies like AI and quantum computing create both opportunities and risks in cybersecurity, and how should businesses adapt? As you answer the questions, take a transdisciplinary approach and consider the different lenses through which you may be viewing this content: Your academic lens – which technology specialism interests you most ? Your lived experience lens – where in the world are you now and what is your previous experience? Your career stage lens - how much work experience do you currently have; are you a career starter, career developer or a career changer? Now reflect on your takeaways from the video: How might the speaker’s perspective impact your professional practice? How can you apply the principles on risk management discussed in the video to improve your own practice or enhance your career development in the tech industry?

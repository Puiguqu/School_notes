# Topic 6 summary

In this topic, we explored the principles of risk management in computing, focusing on how to identify, assess, and mitigate potential risks in computer systems. We examined different types of risks, their likelihood, and their potential impact on security and operations. Understanding these risks is essential for ensuring compliance with industry standards and maintaining secure computing environments. We also delved into cybersecurity, analysing common threats and vulnerabilities that affect information systems. Through case studies and discussions, we evaluated effective risk mitigation strategies, emphasising proactive security measures to protect data and infrastructure. Finally, we covered incident response planning, which involves preparing for and responding to security breaches and system failures. We explored best practices for identifying risks early, managing security incidents efficiently and minimising their impact. By the end of this topic, we gained a comprehensive understanding of risk management in computing, cybersecurity threats, and incident response planning – key areas for maintaining secure and resilient information systems.

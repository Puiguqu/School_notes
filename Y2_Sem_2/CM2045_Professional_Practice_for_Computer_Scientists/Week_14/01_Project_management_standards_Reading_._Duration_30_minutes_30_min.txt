Projects, whether building a bridge or developing a new app, benefit from a structured approach. The Project Management Body of Knowledge (PMBOK) outlines five key project phases that provide a helpful framework: initiating, planning, executing, monitoring and controlling and closing. Understanding these phases is like having a roadmap for your project, ensuring you stay on track and increase your chances of success.

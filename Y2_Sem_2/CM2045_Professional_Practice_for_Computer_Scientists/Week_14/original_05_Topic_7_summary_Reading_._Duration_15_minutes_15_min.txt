# Topic 7 summary

In this topic, we explored the role of industry standards in guiding best practices within the computing profession. We examined the standards set by key organisations such as IEEE and ACM, understanding how they serve as frameworks for professional decision-making and ethical responsibility. These standards provide a structured approach to problem-solving and ensure consistency and quality across the industry. We then analysed real-world case studies to evaluate the importance of adhering to industry standards. Finally, we examined project management standards and security best practices, focusing on how these frameworks are implemented in real-world projects. This helped us connect industry standards to practical applications, particularly in areas such as risk management, cybersecurity and software development. By the end of this topic, we gained insight into how standards help structure the computing profession, ensuring ethical responsibility, security and efficiency in both technical and managerial aspects of the field.

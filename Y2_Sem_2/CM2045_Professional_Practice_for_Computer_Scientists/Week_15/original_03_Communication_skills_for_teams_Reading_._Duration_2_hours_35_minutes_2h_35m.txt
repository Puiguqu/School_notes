Working in teams will always present challenges. This is where we can learn a lot from the fields of psychology, ethnomethodology and more broadly the social sciences. Let us begin to explore some of the fundamentals of how people communicate and then we can move on to more nuanced challenges later. We will start by reading an interesting introduction to the topic which talks about communication as a skilled activity, that you can reflect upon and practise. Hargie, O. The handbook of communication skills. (Oxon: Routledge, 2019) 4th edition Chapter 1.

# Activity: Reflecting on the video
Reading•
. Duration: 10 minutes
10 min - Summary

URL: https://www.coursera.org/learn/uol-cm2045-professional-practice-for-computer-scientists/supplement/GCkE4/activity-reflecting-on-the-video

This activity requires deep reflection on a video featuring Ritvik Shyam and Tanvi Upadhyay from Tata. Viewers are instructed to use a study journal to methodically address a series of complex questions. A foundational question is how appreciating others' expertise can enhance collaboration within a technology team environment. Furthermore, the discussion must tackle the principle of shared accountability, a concept specifically highlighted by Ritvik. Reflective thought is needed regarding how this principle can be practically applied to current or future team projects. Tanvi emphasized the critical role of communication when navigating conflict resolution within a group. Participants must personally reflect on past experiences to analyze when communication was helpful or detrimental to teamwork. From the speakers, insights were gathered regarding the essential characteristics of an effective team member. Based on these traits, participants must identify concrete steps they can take to develop into a stronger team player. Critically, the analysis must adopt a transdisciplinary approach, incorporating multiple academic lenses. The first lens required is the academic lens, prompting identification of the most interesting technology specialism. The second lens is the lived experience lens, which mandates considering one's current global location and previous life experiences. The third analytical dimension is the career stage lens, assessing one's current work experience level or professional status. This status categorization involves identifying whether the individual is a career starter, a career developer, or a career changer. Finally, the reflection must synthesize the speaker’s perspective and determine its potential impact on professional practice. The ultimate goal is to apply the discussed teamwork principles to improve one's own professional practice. This also involves strategies for enhancing overall career development within the competitive tech industry.

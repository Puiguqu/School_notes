Title: Activity: Reflecting on the video | Coursera

After you’ve watched the video with Ritvik Shyam and Tanvi Upadhyay at Tata, reflect on the following questions in your study journal. How can understanding and appreciating the expertise of others improve collaboration in a technology team? Ritvik mentioned that effective teamwork involves shared accountability. How can this principle be applied in your current or future team projects? Tanvi highlighted the importance of communication in conflict resolution. Reflect on a time when communication helped or hindered teamwork—what lessons did you learn? Both speakers discussed what makes a good team member. What steps can you take to develop the traits of a strong team player? As you answer the questions, take a transdisciplinary approach and consider the different lenses through which you may be viewing this content: Your academic lens – which technology specialism interests you most? Your lived experience lens – where in the world are you now and what is your previous experience? Your career stage lens - how much work experience do you currently have; are you a career starter, career developer or a career changer? Consider the following takeaways. How might the speaker’s perspective impact your professional practice? How can you apply the principles on teamwork discussed in the video to improve your own practice or enhance your career development in the tech industry?

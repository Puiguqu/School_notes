In this topic, we explored the principles of effective teamwork in computing, focusing on strategies for collaboration and communication. We examined what makes a good teammate and how to navigate challenges that arise, including how to communicate constructively with other people. We created heuristics for good teamwork, which you can bring forward and use in future teamwork scenarios. We also examined teamwork in agile environments, looking at how agile methodologies support effective collaboration in computing projects. By the end of this topic, we developed a deeper understanding of how to work effectively in teams, manage collaboration challenges, and apply best practices to computing projects.

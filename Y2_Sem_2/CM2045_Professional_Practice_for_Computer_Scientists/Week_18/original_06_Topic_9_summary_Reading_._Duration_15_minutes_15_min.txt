# Topic 9 summary
Reading•
. Duration: 15 minutes

URL: https://www.coursera.org/learn/uol-cm2045-professional-practice-for-computer-scientists/supplement/3XZMF/topic-9-summary

In this topic, we explored the fundamentals of project management in computing, focusing on structured approaches to planning, executing and monitoring team-based projects. We introduced key project management methodologies, such as Scrum and Kanban, examining how these frameworks support organisation, efficiency and adaptability in computing projects. We also introduced Agile project management, looking at how iterative development and flexible workflows contribute to project success. Through real-world case studies, we identified the challenges and best practices involved in implementing Agile versus traditional methodologies, highlighting how they can either enable success or contribute to failure depending on how they are applied. By the end of this topic, we developed a solid understanding of project management techniques and how they can be applied to computing projects to improve efficiency, teamwork and overall project success.

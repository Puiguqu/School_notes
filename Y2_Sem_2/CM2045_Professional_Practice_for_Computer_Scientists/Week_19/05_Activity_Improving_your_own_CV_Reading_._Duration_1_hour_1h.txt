# Activity: Improving your own CV
Reading•
. Duration: 1 hour
1h - Summary

URL: https://www.coursera.org/learn/uol-cm2045-professional-practice-for-computer-scientists/supplement/C5e9O/activity-improving-your-own-cv

Students are encouraged to use this opportunity to either create or thoroughly update their personal curriculum vitae (CV). They should utilize the digital tools and comprehensive resources provided by the University of London Careers Service. Accessing the suite of CV-enhancement resources is critical for effective job hunting. This includes reviewing current CV-enhancement webinars and guidelines. Furthermore, specialized AI-enhanced tools are available through the platform called Careers Connect. The University of London Careers Service has meticulously identified key steps necessary for building a successful CV. The first crucial step is to begin by referencing a specific job specification. An effective CV mandates that the applicant carefully reads and directly responds to the skills sought by the recruiter. It is advised to check the available vacancy feed on the University's portal for suitable roles. Personalized feedback is most useful when the applicant applies for a specific job vacancy. This focus enables the use of digital resources to rigorously evaluate the targeting of the CV for that particular role. The second vital rule concerns the adoption of active language within the CV. To best convey professional achievements, users must employ active phrasing. Examples of appropriate active terms include ‘I achieved,’ ‘I developed,’ ‘I initiated,’ or ‘I improved.’ Conversely, applicants should strictly avoid passive language structures. Phrases like ‘I had to,’ ‘I was involved in,’ and ‘I was required to’ should be avoided entirely. The third major step involves thoroughly brainstorming all potential skills. These skills should be gathered from various sources. Sources include participation in the current module, the remainder of the degree program, professional work, internships, and volunteering efforts. Applicants must then match these personal skills with the requirements listed in the job advertisement. It is particularly important to reflect exact skill words found in the job details. Regarding formatting, the CV should adhere to a length constraint of no more than two pages. Once the CV is created or enhanced, the student can leverage the AI-enhanced CV evaluation tools on Careers Connect. These tools allow for continuous improvement and iterative refinement of the document. Users can upload job descriptions, CVs, and covering letters for feedback. This feedback cycle can be performed repeatedly, enabling ongoing adjustment. This process ensures the CV’s targeting remains relevant across different roles while the student is actively enrolled. Finally, should any links featured on this page prove to be broken, students must promptly report the issue via the Student Portal.

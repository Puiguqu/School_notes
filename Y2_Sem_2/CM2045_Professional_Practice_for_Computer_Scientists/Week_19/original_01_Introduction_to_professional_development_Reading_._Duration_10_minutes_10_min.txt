Welcome to Topic 10! This topic will be led by Liz Wilkinson from the University of London Careers Service. In this topic, you will explore professional development, focusing on: career planning, including identifying your priorities and available career paths how to showcase your strengths and experience using your CV and online profiles building a professional network. Remember that whether you are at the start of your career journey (Career Starter), already have work-based experience (Career Developer), or are looking to change your career (Career Changer), The University of London Careers Service is here to help all students wherever you are in your career journey and wherever you are based in the world.

Were there any moments where you found it difficult to balance creativity and efficiency? How did considering different user roles like performers, producers or agents change the way you thought about the design? Did you add colour? Metrics? Reflection helps solidify these insights and allows you to apply them in a broader context. Efficiency might be pivotal in the context of a financial trading system, where seconds could mean millions of pounds. For scientific applications, effectiveness might be a priority even at the cost of speed. Software design is strange in that there's always a trade-off.

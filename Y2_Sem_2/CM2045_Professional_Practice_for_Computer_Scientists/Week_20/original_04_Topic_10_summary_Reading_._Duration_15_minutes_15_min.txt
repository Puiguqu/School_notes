# Topic 10 summary

In this final topic, we have focused on career planning and the steps needed to build a successful career in the computing field. We explored strategies for professional development and reflected on your career priorities and the paths open to you. A key part of this topic was learning how to craft effective online portfolios, including optimising CVs, LinkedIn profiles and GitHub profiles. We discussed best practices for showcasing skills and experience in a way that attracts potential employers and opportunities. We also examined the role of networking in career development, looking at strategies for building meaningful professional connections. By the end of this topic, you developed a better understanding of how to present ourselves professionally, build a strong network and take proactive steps toward career success in computing.

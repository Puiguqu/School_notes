# Overcoming fear and presenting with confidence
Reading•
. Duration: 15 minutes
15 min

URL: https://www.coursera.org/learn/uol-cm2045-professional-practice-for-computer-scientists/supplement/mziSt/overcoming-fear-and-presenting-with-confidence

This video explores why we feel nervous when speaking in public and the brain’s ‘fight or flight’ response that causes physical symptoms such as shaking or a dry mouth. You'll also learn practical techniques to manage these reactions and speak with confidence. If you are unable to access the video please access the reading listed below. Watch The science of stage fright (and how to overcome it) Edwards V. V. ‘Stage fright: How to overcome stage it in 15 easy steps’, Science of People (2024). If you are unable to access the videos, please read the text on this page: Torgovnick, K. 'Required watching for any TED speaker: the science of stage fright', TEDBlog (2013).

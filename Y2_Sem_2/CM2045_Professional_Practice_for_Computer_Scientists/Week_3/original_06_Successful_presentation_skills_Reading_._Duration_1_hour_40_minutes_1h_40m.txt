# Successful presentation skills

. Duration: 1 hour 40 minutes

Presentations are not just about your ability to communicate as much information as possible in a short time period. They are about creating compelling narratives. Take my lectures for example. They are far from all encompassing. Watching a five minute video is unlikely to make you an expert. However, it introduces you to a novel idea and pique's your interest. A good presentation achieves its purpose. Whether that is to inform, persuade, describe or some other purpose entirely. To do this it has to first engage and captivate the audience. Let us explore this in more detail with the following short reading activity. Bradbury, A. Successful presentation skills. (London: Kogan Page, 2016) pp. 3–18.

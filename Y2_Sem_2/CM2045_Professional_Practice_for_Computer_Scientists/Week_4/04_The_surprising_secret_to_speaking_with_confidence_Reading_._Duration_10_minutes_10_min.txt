Further reflection should center on the story of Demosthenes, a historical figure known for his oratory skills. Analyzing this narrative helps students understand how consistent practice overcomes difficulties or professional setbacks.

The speaker introduces a key biological detail by highlighting the crucial role of the diaphragm in managing anxiety. This shifts the focus from purely mental skills to physical physiology. Students are asked to consider how their physical state influences their emotional and mental state.

Applying this awareness is highly valuable for personal and professional life enhancements. The module concludes by focusing on actionable techniques for improved presence.

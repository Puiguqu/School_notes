# Activity – Implementing inclusive practices
Reading•
. Duration: 30 minutes
30 min

URL: https://www.coursera.org/learn/uol-cm2045-professional-practice-for-computer-scientists/supplement/xBXTH/activity-implementing-inclusive-practices

In this activity, you will think critically about inclusivity in web development and apply your knowledge to a practical scenario. You will analyse a hypothetical situation, identify potential inclusivity issues and propose solutions that demonstrate your understanding of accessibility, usability and ethical considerations. Scenario: You are a front-end developer working on a new e-commerce website for a clothing retailer. The website aims to reach a diverse audience, including people with disabilities, users from different cultural backgrounds and individuals with varying levels of technical expertise. Your task is to ensure that the website is inclusive and accessible to everyone. You can use your study journal to complete the following tasks. Tasks: Identify potential inclusivity issues: Identify potential areas where the website could exclude or create barriers for certain users. Consider aspects such as: accessibility cultural sensitivity usability code quality. Propose inclusive solutions: Propose specific solutions to address the identified inclusivity issues. Consider the following: Accessibility standards: Adherence to WCAG guidelines (e.g. providing alternative text for images, ensuring keyboard navigation, using sufficient colour contrast). Assistive technologies: Compatibility with screen readers, voice recognition software and other assistive technologies. Responsive design: Adapting the layout and functionality for different devices and screen sizes. Internationalisation and localisation: Supporting multiple languages and cultural conventions. Plain language and clear communication: Using simple and concise language, avoiding jargon and technical terms. Diverse representation: Ensuring images and content reflect the diversity of the target audience. Justify your choices: Explain why your proposed solutions are effective in promoting inclusivity and accessibility. Refer to relevant concepts and best practices. Example: Issue: The website uses a complex grid layout that may be difficult for users with visual impairments to navigate using a screen reader. Solution: Ensure the website's code uses semantic HTML5 elements to structure the content logically. Provide Accessible rich internet attributes (ARIA) to enhance accessibility for assistive technologies. Consider offering a simplified layout option for users who prefer it. Justification: Semantic HTML5 and ARIA attributes improve the website's compatibility with screen readers, making it easier for users with visual impairments to understand the content and navigate the site. Offering a simplified layout option caters to users with cognitive disabilities or those who prefer a less visually complex design.

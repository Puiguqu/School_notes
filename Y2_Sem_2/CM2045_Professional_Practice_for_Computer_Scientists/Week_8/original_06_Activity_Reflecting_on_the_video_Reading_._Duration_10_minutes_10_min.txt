Title: Activity: Reflecting on the video | Coursera

After you’ve watched the video with Lilian Balatsou (the Conversational AI Lead for Vodafone), reflect on the following questions in your study journal. How can companies ensure that their design processes effectively incorporate the perspectives of diverse stakeholders to reduce bias in technology? What challenges might organisations face when implementing inclusive computing solutions, and how can they address these challenges effectively? In what ways can organisations balance technological innovation with ethical considerations to support equity and inclusivity in workplace technologies? As you answer the questions, take a transdisciplinary approach and consider the different lenses through which you may be viewing this content: Your academic lens – which technology specialism interests you most? Your lived experience lens – where in the world are you now and what is your previous experience? Your career stage lens - how much work experience do you currently have; are you a career starter, career developer or a career changer? Then consider your takeaways from the video: How might the speaker’s perspective impact your professional practice? How can you apply the principles of inclusive computing and ethical technology discussed in the video to improve your own practice or enhance your career development in the tech industry?

This module provides a very warm welcome and initial guidance for incoming students. To begin, familiarize yourself thoroughly with the module structure and the tasks awaiting you in the forthcoming weeks. It is crucial to read the complete module syllabus to understand the module's overall scope. Students should also watch the module introduction video to grasp what the course material entails. Reviewing the learning outcomes, which are found in the syllabus, summarizes the key knowledge and skills you must demonstrate upon completion. Furthermore, examining the assessment section of the syllabus details exactly how you will achieve a passing grade. If this is your first time using the platform, or if you wish to deepen your understanding of Coursera's tools, take the Orientation course. During this Orientation course, you will learn about three essential communication tools used throughout the program: discussion forums, Slack, and Zoom. It is important to say 'Hello' to your classmates by posting in the designated ‘Introduce yourself’ discussion forum. Before participating in general forums, review the specific guidelines on using discussion forums, which are available in the Orientation course. The Orientation course also offers detailed information about attending live webinars. Additionally, familiarize yourself with the Online Library, as its tutorial provides a wealth of essential resources for your studies. Within the Orientation course, you will be introduced to Studiosity, a new online service the University is piloting. Studiosity is a valuable resource designed to support your academic writing, offering help with topics needing review. The University of London Careers Service assists all students, regardless of their current career status. This support is available whether you are a career starter, a career developer, or a career changer. On the Orientation course, you can read more about enhancing career prospects and the services provided by the Careers Service. Aligning your academic study with your personal career goals is key to staying motivated and successful. Students can start this process by visiting the Careers Connect platform. Furthermore, attendance at the live targeted BSc Computer Science careers webinars is highly encouraged throughout the program. For those new to their professional journey, the module provides insights on developing work experience in a video format. Specific career starter resources are available for those who wish to explore this topic further. We hope that all students enjoy the module and look forward to seeing your active contributions.

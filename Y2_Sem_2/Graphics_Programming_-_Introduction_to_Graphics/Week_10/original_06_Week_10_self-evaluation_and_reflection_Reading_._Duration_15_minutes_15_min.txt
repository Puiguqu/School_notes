# Week 10 self-evaluation and reflection

. Duration: 15 minutes
15 min

As you progress through your course, it is crucial to regularly assess your understanding and capabilities against the learning outcomes. This exercise is designed to help you reflect on your learning journey, identify areas where you may need to deepen your knowledge or skills, and develop a plan for improvement. Use this checklist to evaluate your understanding of the lessons covered in Week 10. If you don't feel confident about any of these concepts/tasks, revisit the relevant lecture videos, readings and activities to help you consolidate your knowledge. 1. Understanding Procedural Generation in Games I can clearly explain what procedural generation is. I understand how procedural generation is used in game design. I can list the pros and cons of procedural generation in games. 2. Exploring Generative Art and Design I can discuss generative art and how it is created. I can describe how procedural techniques apply to both art and design. I can identify at least one practical application of generative art outside gaming. 3. Connecting Concepts with Andy Lomas' Work I can explain how Andy Lomas’ work links to procedural generation. I can draw connections between Lomas' artistic approach and the syllabus content. I can discuss specific examples from Lomas' work that reflect key concepts covered in the module. 4. Reflecting on Key Insights from Interviews I can summarize key insights from Andy Lomas’ interview. I can summarize key insights from Alan Zucconi’s interview. I understand how their perspectives contribute to my knowledge of generative art and procedural generation. 5. Applying Knowledge to Broader Contexts I feel confident explaining how procedural generation and generative art influence fields beyond gaming, such as film and art. I can discuss the impact of generative processes on design and fabrication. I can provide examples of procedural generation or generative art that inspire me or enhance my understanding of the topic. 6. Self-Reflection I can assess my strengths and areas for improvement in understanding procedural generation and generative art. I feel confident about discussing and applying these concepts in future projects. I have identified any additional resources or topics I need to review for a deeper understanding.
